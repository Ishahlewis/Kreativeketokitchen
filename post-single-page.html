FAQ

How do I get my order?

We use NZ couriers, strictly overnight delivery nationwide, non rural.


What are the sweeteners?

I use erythritol in all my sweet items.


What is erythritol?

Erythritol is a naturally occuring sugar alcohol (polyol) found in fruits and fermented foods and is used as a sugar substitute. The sweetness is approximately 70% of cane sugar.

Can I pick up?

Yes you can, However I have a day job and am not always in. If you would like to pick up, this can be organised upon placing your order.


How long will my loaves and goodies last?

Loaves can be stored in the fridge for up to a week. If planning on freezing - transfer to a zip lock bag and pull out slice by slice. It will last for 3 weeks in the freezer, as will cookies and brownies. Freezing avoids the temptation of eating all at once!
