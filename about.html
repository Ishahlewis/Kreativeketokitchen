About

Kreative Keto

Kreative Keto Kitchen began when I personally discovered on my own Keto journey that there are not enough bread and goodie options out there. The time it takes and the ingredients out there are at a premium cost. I buy in bulk the freshest of the fresh keto ingredients - seeds, sugar free and healthy eats. All your loaves and treats are made to order and are available at any time.

I am currently based in Remuera, Auckland. It is just me baking and kreating and sales executive during the day, baker at night. All items are packaged with care using compostable, bleach free bio degradable packaging. Give my bread and treats a try, you will be hooked!
